• What does WASD Vision do?
  • Displays an on-screen keyboard and mouse for video game streamers.
  • Does it do anything else?
  • No.
  • Will this install anything on my computer?
  • No.
  • Will this keep track of what I type?
  • No.
  • Can I show the keyboard without the mouse?
  • Yes. OBS recognizes the keyboard and mouse as separate windows.
  • What if I accidentally type sensitive information while displaying WASD Vision?
  • WASD Vision will pause for 30 seconds if you type the beginning of a protected word. You may add any words you'd like to protect to the protected words list.
  • How do I update the color, keyboard layout, or protected words?
  • Right-click on the tray icon and select Options.

  • You can find instructions on the Download page.
